About EV Guide UK

Independent, expert guidance to help every UK driver make the switch to electric with confidence — and without the jargon.

Our Story

Born from a Genuine Need

We built the guide we wished existed when we were researching our own first EV.

EV Guide UK was founded in 2022 by a group of UK motorists who had each spent months wading through conflicting manufacturer claims, outdated forum posts, and advice written for US drivers. We wanted honest, up-to-date information tailored specifically to the UK experience — our roads, our charging infrastructure, our grants, and our weather.

What started as a small independent blog has grown into one of the UK's most-visited EV resources, reaching over 40,000 drivers every month. We cover everything from first-time buyer questions to in-depth comparisons for experienced EV enthusiasts.

We are completely independent. We do not accept payments from manufacturers to influence our reviews, and we clearly label any affiliate relationships. Our only goal is to help you make the best decision for your situation.
